Zachary "Z" Clark

Z is an up-and-coming Cybersecurity specialist who has had an interest in computers from a young age. He started from building computers to learning more about how they function from a software standpoint and found a particular fondness in learning how others have found exploits in the way computers or users function to gain access to parts of computers they otherwise would not have had access to. He continues even now to further his knowledge to help protect people from the dangers that computers and people can hold.
